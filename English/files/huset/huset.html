BO100 – the hosue, the area, the street

A Jewel with Heritage

BO100 is located at Monbijougatan 4, a central location in Malmö, just one block away from Triangeln. The street got its name in 1889 and its current stretch from Norra Skolgatan to Folkets park in 1904.
       The name Monbijou comes from the innkeeper Christian Gottlieb Amilon, who gave this name to an outdoor inn in the area around 1810. The name may have been inspired by the Monbijou Palace in Berlin.
       At the inn, located at the corner of Spångatan/Norra Skolgatan, there was a dance floor and an amusement park. During the 1850s, it served as a cholera hospital, and in 1879, Monbijouskolan was built on the site.
    Mon Bijou is a French term meaning "my jewel". The name is fitting for the area, which has been a jewel in Malmö's history and development over the years.


A Hundred Percent Good Housing

BO100 stands for "one hundred percent good housing" and is the result of a unique collaboration between Malmö architect Ivo Waldhör and the original 39 tenants. The project was a creative idea that arose after a traditional housing fair in 1986. The Tenants' Association in Malmö had experienced that the housing fair had become more of a regular building product fair than an opportunity to present innovative ideas.

One Color for All

-> The building has 39 apartments ranging from 42 to 140 square meters.
-> Three staircases. Four to five floors.
-> All apartments have their own floor plan, there are no two apartments that look alike.
-> Six apartments are built on two levels.
-> Five of the apartments have walk-in pantries.
-> Nineteen apartments have open-plan kitchens.
-> Marble window sills.
-> The building has 323 windows distributed among 40 different window shapes (letter). Each apartment has its own color for the window frames.
-> Security doors with their own color scheme.


Common Areas

-> Garage with 19 parking spaces in the basement floor.
-> Common room for meetings, activities, and parties.
-> Sauna with sun terrace.
-> Guest room with three beds.
-> Hobby room in the basement.
-> Gym.
-> Attic room for ping pong and some storage.
-> Laundry rooms in each stairwell.

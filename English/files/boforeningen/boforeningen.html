Living in BO100

The Tenant Association BO100

In BO100, it's not just tenants who live, but an entire housing association that collectively takes responsibility for the operation of the property and its apartments. The housing association BO100 serves as an intermediary between the tenants and the property owner MKB Fastighets AB, and is responsible for leasing the apartments to its members.
   One advantage of this type of housing is that members of the housing association have the opportunity to influence their rent by participating in various tasks such as cleaning, maintenance, and administration. This provides a unique opportunity for residents to participate in decisions that affect their living environment and also impact their finances. In this way, members become more involved in the operation of the property and can contribute to a pleasant and functional living environment.
   To become a tenant in BO100, no deposit is required, which means that it is more accessible to a broader group of people. Additionally, it is not allowed to sell or demand a deposit from a new tenant upon entry, creating a safe and stable living situation for all members of the association.
   As a member of the housing association BO100, one has a unique opportunity to participate in decision-making processes and actively influence their living environment. By collaborating with other members and participating in various tasks, one can contribute to a pleasant and functional living environment while reducing individual costs. This also creates a strong sense of community and belonging among residents of BO100, contributing to a safe and harmonious living environment.

Contact BO100

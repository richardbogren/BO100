Contact BO100

Use the contact form below if you want to get in touch with the Association BO100.

-> The Association can receive study visits from organizations and associations, but please contact us well in advance with the purpose and wishes regarding such a study visit, as this needs to be planned in advanced.

-> Media who want to do a report about the building are also welcome to contact us via the form below.

-> Anyone can apply for membership in the Housing Association BO100. The Association has its own housing queue and vacant apartments can be allocated to members of the association.

-> You can express your interest in becoming a member by submitting the form below. When you become a member, you have the opportunity to apply for an apartment by filling out an application.

-> Note that BO100.se is run by the residents of the building, and therefore it may sometimes take time before a response can be given.
